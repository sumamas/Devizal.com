Website Development FAQs

Answers to common questions about our website development services

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. A basic brochure website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex websites or web applications can take 8-12 weeks or longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your requirements.

What technologies do you use for website development?

We use a variety of technologies depending on project requirements, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, PHP, Laravel, WordPress, and more. We choose the best stack for your specific needs.

Do you provide website hosting?

Yes, we offer reliable hosting solutions as part of our services. We can also help you migrate an existing website to our hosting or set up your website on your preferred hosting provider.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we develop are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. We test on various screen sizes to ensure perfect display and functionality.

Do you provide maintenance after launch?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to keep your website updated, secure, and performing at its best. This includes security updates, content updates, performance optimization, and technical support.
